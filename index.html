I've learned a lot in the classroom, but I've also worked on projects at hackathons where I collaborated with my friends. Visit the Projects section of this page to see some of my favorite projects I've worked on in the past.

I have experiences using languages like Python, Java, C++, C#, MySQL, HTML, and CSS, as well as technologies and tools like TensorFlow, Unity, Google Cloud Platform, and Amazon Web Services. Outside of technical ability, I also have great teamwork and communication skills gained from my experience in hackathon groups, my time working at the front desk of Arroyo Vista student housing, and my two years of experience as a board member for the KKAP dance team.

As previously mentioned, I also served as a board member for another dance team called KKAP for two years, where I worked in a team of 10 student leaders to organize weekly dance workshops, dance practices, and social events for over 100 members.
